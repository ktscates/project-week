Working at Aperto - the IBM iX Studio Berlin

We are Aperto - the IBM iX Studio in Berlin! Since 2016, we have been part of IBM iX, one of the
largest digital agencies in the world. As part of the IBM iX agency family in Germany and Europe, we
are constantly growing and developing. With new topics, innovations, services and thousands of
colleagues worldwide. Together with IBM, we believe in shaping a better digitalised world. How
would it be if you played a decisive role in this?

We are always looking for top people. To make them feel at home, we create an atmosphere in which
all colleagues can develop to the best of their abilities. To achieve this, we offer regular training,
flexible working hours and an inspiring environment right in Berlin-Mitte. We come together in the
morning for breakfast and in the evening for a cool drink at the bar. During the current challenging
pandemic period, we offer our employees everything from laptops to digital tools to office chairs, so
that working in the home office works as well as in the office. We also believe that the best results
are achieved as a team. Open, collaborative and agile.

You want to help us make the digital world a little better? Then take a look at our job postings to see
if there’s something suitable for you. If so, let us know so we can get to know each other!

You’re also welcome to take a look behind the scenes - of course, you can find Aperto on
LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter and Facebook.

You can find out more about IBM iX and our global studio network at: ibmix.de
